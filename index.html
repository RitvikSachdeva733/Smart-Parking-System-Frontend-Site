Integrated Smart Parking Management System

What is SSPS?

It's a go-to for hassle-free parking! A smart solution that keeps traffic snarls in parking spots a thing of the past. Parking just got a whole lot easier. Suitable for all types of parking areas, it digitizes end-to end parking processes including multi-tenant, multi-level parking.

Top Features of Our Smart Parking System

Customize Spaces

Customize & reserve parking space as per company, staff, pay-&-park, etc.

Slots Per Vehicle Type

Allocate slots as per two-wheeler and four-wheeler vehicles to utilise all available space.

View Live Reports

No more manual checking of empty space. View it on your phone. Also view live reports.

Auto-Generated Passes

QR code-based passes are instantly auto-generated for a visitor.

On-Spot Or Monthly Pay

Parking passes generated can be on pay-&-park or a monthly pay basis. It is FASTag enabled.

Access Control System

Integrate parking passes with access control system - boom barriers, biometrics, etc.

Slots On LED Display

Display empty slot info such as floor and bay on LED screens in the parking bay.

Ensure Periodic Patrolling

Monitor security guards on duty and shift changes in the parking bay area.

Levy Penalty for Violations

Levy instant penalty tickets, if any person or vehicle is found guilty of any time or rule violation.

FAQs

What is a parking management system?

A parking management system transforms how parking works, making full use of every space and smoothing out the whole process. It offers instant insights into parking, like how many spots are open, where you can find them, options for reserved spots, and straightforward payment methods, alongside detailed analytics and various other functionalities, all designed to enhance your parking journey.

Is a parking management system standalone software or hardware?

It can go either route. A smart car parking system brings them both together. Semantix's solution is pretty clever, combining user-friendly visitor management software with reliable access control hardware.

What is a “smart” car parking system?

A “smart” car parking system is a system that completely digitizes the whole parking process from start to finish. And it's all powered by technologies like mobile, cloud, and IoT.

Who should use Semantix parking management system?

Our smart parking system is versatile, fitting for any parking area. Specifically tailored for multi-tenant, multi-level car parking spaces, it's ideal for providers or owners seeking streamlined and efficient parking processes. If you're looking to fast-track your parking operations, Semantix's parking management system is the way to go.

What is unique about Semantix parking management system?

Our parking system is a smart software seamlessly integrated with both visitor management system and access control hardware.
